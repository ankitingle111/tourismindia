Kedarnath

Kedarnath is a town located in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. It holds immense religious significance and is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites in Hinduism.

Location

Kedarnath is situated in the Himalayas at an elevation of approximately 3,583 meters (11,755 feet) above sea level.

Kedarnath Temple

The Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is the most prominent attraction in the town. It is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas and holds great reverence among devotees.

Accessibility

Due to its high altitude and remote location, reaching Kedarnath involves a trek of approximately 16 kilometers from Gaurikund or can be accessed via helicopter services during certain months.

Natural Beauty

Besides its religious importance, Kedarnath is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, surrounded by snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, and pristine rivers.
