New Mumbai

New Mumbai, also known as Navi Mumbai, is a planned satellite city located on the mainland of Maharashtra, India. It was developed to ease the congestion in Mumbai and has grown into one of the largest planned cities in the world.

Planning and Development

New Mumbai was planned and developed in 1972 to accommodate the growing population and commercial activities of Mumbai. The city is divided into sectors, each with its own infrastructure and amenities.

Infrastructure

New Mumbai boasts modern infrastructure, including wide roads, well-planned residential and commercial complexes, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and recreational areas.

Economy

The city has a diverse economy with industries such as information technology, manufacturing, and services. It also serves as a major hub for trade and commerce in the region.

Transportation

New Mumbai is well-connected to Mumbai and other parts of Maharashtra through road and rail networks. It also has its own local train service, known as the Navi Mumbai Suburban Railway.
