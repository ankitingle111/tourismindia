Ayodhya

Ayodhya is a city located in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is known for its rich cultural and historical significance.

History

Ayodhya is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama, a central figure in the Hindu epic Ramayana. It has been a sacred city for Hindus for thousands of years.

Landmarks

  • Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple
  • Hanuman Garhi
  • Kanak Bhawan
  • Swarg Dwar

Cultural Significance

Ayodhya is a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus and attracts millions of devotees each year, especially during festivals like Diwali and Ram Navami.

Tourism

Aside from its religious importance, Ayodhya also offers various tourist attractions, including historical sites, temples, and beautiful ghats along the Sarayu River.
