About Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant blend of history and modernity. From ancient monuments like the Red Fort and Qutub Minar to bustling markets like Chandni Chowk, Delhi offers a rich tapestry of experiences for visitors.

Places to Visit

  • Red Fort
  • Qutub Minar
  • India Gate
  • Lotus Temple
  • Akshardham Temple
  • Jama Masjid
  • Humayun's Tomb
  • Chandni Chowk
  • Hauz Khas Village
  • Lodhi Gardens

Things to Do

  • Explore the bustling markets
  • Take a heritage walk
  • Enjoy street food
  • Attend a cultural show
  • Visit museums and art galleries
  • Experience Delhi's nightlife

Getting Around

Delhi has a well-connected metro system, as well as buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws for getting around the city.
